UPSC Prelims Exam Date Announced
URGENT

Updated: Today | Category: UPSC

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has officially announced the examination schedule for 2024. The UPSC Civil Services Prelims exam will be conducted on June 16, 2024. Candidates must register before the deadline to appear for the exam.

Key Details:

  • Exam Date: June 16, 2024

  • Total Vacancies: 1025 (Tentative)

SSC CGL Exam Pattern Revised
Update

Updated: 2 days ago | Category: SSC

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has made important changes to the exam pattern for CGL (Combined Graduate Level) examination. The new pattern includes additional questions and extended duration for Tier-I exam.

Changes:

  • Tier-I: Duration increased to 90 minutes (was 60 minutes)
  • Total Questions: 100 (was 100, but with new type distribution)
  • Negative Marking: 0.5 mark per wrong answer
  • Exam Dates: July 8-10, 2024
